About Caesar Cipher

The Caesar Cipher is one of the simplest encryption techniques. It shifts each letter by a fixed number of positions in the alphabet.

Example:
Message: "HELLO"   Shift: 3
Result: "KHOOR"   (H→K, E→H, L→O, L→O, O→R)

If a letter goes past 'Z', it wraps around to the beginning of the alphabet.

About Rail Fence Cipher

      
The Rail Fence Cipher writes the message in a zigzag pattern across a number of rails (rows), then reads off each row to produce the ciphertext.

      

        Example (3 rails):

        Message: "WEAREDISCOVEREDRUNATONCE"

        Rail 1: W . . . E . . . I . . . V . . . D . . . V . . .

        Rail 2: . E . R . D . S . O . E . E . R . N . A . O . C .

        Rail 3: . . A . . . I . . . C . . . . . U . . . T . . . E

        Result: "WECRLTEERDSOEEFEAABORADICVNE"
